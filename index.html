About me

Built from the
ground up.

I'm a Computer Science B. Sc. degree holder from Lebanese University - Faculty of Sciences (Class of 2026, GPA 83/100) based in Tripoli, Lebanon. My background spans the full vertical of software; from bare-metal assembly and Linux internals all the way to web platforms, databases, and interactive graphics.

I'm drawn to problems that require understanding how things actually work, not just how to use them. That's led me to build several projects, each with a different set of constraints, and each built with care.

Outside of engineering, I work as a junior independent computer science teacher, instructing university peers and early-stage students in advanced data structures, systems programming in C, programming paradigms, structural networking fundamentals and various other subjects. I speak Arabic natively, English fluently, and French well. I'm looking for environments where craft is valued and depth is respected.

As a dedicated and hard-working professional, I am driven by a commitment to giving my absolute best to every project, consistently delivering high-quality results. I blend strong critical thinking and analytical problem-solving skills with high learning velocity, allowing me to master new technologies and adapt to changing environments with agility. A collaborative teammate at heart, I leverage strong communication and emotional intelligence to foster a positive, cohesive team dynamic. Furthermore, my solid understanding of project management principles ensures that I remain organized, meet tight deadlines, and effectively channel collective efforts toward achieving successful project outcomes.
